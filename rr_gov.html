Overview
Concept Summary
This document presents a concept for organizing a cross-functional team into a structured association capable of delivering a coordinated, rapid-response supply chain. The association operates through a tiered governance model in which a Steering Committee provides strategic oversight and working groups execute operational scope across defined supply chain functions. Membership is restricted to operators active on the African continent and is subject to a formal vetting and onboarding process.

The association is designed to function as both a coordinating body and a data aggregation entity, enabling member organizations to contribute to a bioscience global intelligence system while maintaining enforceable contractual obligations and equitable access to funding.
